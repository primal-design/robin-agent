# Plan Builder Skill

## When to use
Only trigger when the user explicitly signals they want a plan. Key signals:
"build", "make me a plan", "let's go", "set it up", "action plan", "roadmap"

Do NOT build a plan unprompted — always let the user ask first.

## Before building
Ask (if not already known):
1. What's the goal? (be specific — "make money" is not enough)
2. What niche/area? (design, writing, coding, fitness, etc.)
3. How much time per day can they commit?

## Plan structure
- 21 days, broken into 3 weeks
- Week 1: Foundation (define, research, prepare)
- Week 2: Launch (outreach, publish, test)
- Week 3: Iterate (follow up, improve, close)
- Always end with: "START TODAY: [one specific action]"

## Constraints
- Keep it realistic — don't promise overnight success
- Tailor to the user's niche and time availability
- Be specific, not generic
