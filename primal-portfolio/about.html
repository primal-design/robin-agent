About

Recruitment experience shaped by operations, candidate support, and practical AI thinking.

I help candidates present themselves better, and I help recruitment workflows become clearer, faster, and more useful.

I'm Primal J Fernando - a Recruitment Professional based in London, building AI-powered career solutions to help South Asian professionals succeed in the UK job market.

My career began in international recruitment, where I spent six years leading operations for a government-licensed agency supporting employers across the UAE and Qatar. I managed candidate sourcing, screening, interview coordination, compliance, visa processing and employer relationships.

After moving to the UK, I combined hands-on workplace experience with practical AI workflows to support job seekers with CV optimisation, interview preparation and application strategy. That work became FEN - an AI-powered career support platform that helps candidates present their skills clearly and confidently to UK employers.

I'm open to roles and collaborations in Recruitment Operations, Talent Acquisition, Candidate Experience, People Operations and recruitment technology.
